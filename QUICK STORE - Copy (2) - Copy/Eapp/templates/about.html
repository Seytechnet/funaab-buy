Welcome to FUNAAB BUY

We connect students within FUNAAB, providing a platform for buying and selling products conveniently and safely.

FUNAAB Buy is a student-driven marketplace where you can find unique and affordable items directly from your peers. Our platform aims to simplify buying and selling within the university by offering a place for students to list their items, negotiate deals, and arrange meetups for safe transactions. We don’t charge any fees and encourage in-person pickups or public meetups to ensure trust and safety. Our mission is to foster a supportive community where students can both earn and save money while contributing to a more sustainable campus economy.
